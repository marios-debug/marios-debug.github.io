# BTS Cloud Computing — Reflection Report

## Overview
Starting the BTS Cloud Computing course marks a real new chapter for me. It is more demanding, with higher expectations, and it pushes me to become more independent and consistent. I am stepping into IT without a prior background, so every lesson is a learning opportunity.

---

## SWOT Analysis

### Strengths
- Curious and eager to learn
- Adaptable and able to integrate into a group
- Strong teamwork skills
- Good observation and attention to detail

### Weaknesses
- No prior IT background
- Lack of confidence, especially in competitive environments
- Difficulty maintaining focus and tendency to procrastinate
- Forgetfulness — I need to take notes regularly
- Low energy levels at times (sleep-related)
- Communication difficulties under pressure (anxiety, language barriers)
- Lack of autonomy and organizational skills

### Opportunities
- Learning project management and business skills
- Internship opportunities in IT and cloud infrastructure
- Exposure to professional environments and external experts
- Supportive class and teaching staff

### Threats / Challenges
- Limited IT knowledge makes some lessons hard to follow
- Being new to the field means catching up takes extra effort
- Need to build autonomy and organizational skills quickly

---

## Progress & Actions Taken
I am actively working on improving my weaknesses. I now plan my week in advance, break tasks into smaller steps, and track my progress so I can stay calm and move forward steadily. I have learned that good documentation and communication are not "extra" — they are part of the job, just like the technical work.

---

## Conclusion
This portfolio shows my progress, my projects, and my mindset: learning step by step, testing, improving, and staying motivated. I am proud of how far I have come, and I am excited to keep building my skills and growing in this field.
