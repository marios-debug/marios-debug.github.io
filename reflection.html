Part 1 - Reflection & Progress

Reflection & Progress

A concise overview of growth, learning, and next steps during the BTS Cloud Computing journey.

Progress

What changed since the start of the program.

  • From basic concepts to confidently building and documenting cloud workflows.
  • Improved project structure: clearer goals, better tool selection, and results-focused summaries.
  • Learned to communicate technical ideas through visuals, checklists, and short reflections.
  • More consistency in planning, time management, and revision habits.

Milestones

Key achievements and turning points.

  • Delivered Power Automate and Power Apps prototypes with documented steps.
  • Completed PROMA project phases and presented results with evidence.
  • Built a smart technologies project summary with tools, architecture, and outcomes.

Challenges

Areas that needed extra effort.

  • Balancing technical work with clear documentation.
  • Choosing the most relevant evidence for each requirement.
  • Keeping momentum during complex or long tasks.

Aha moments

Insights that accelerated progress.

  • Visuals (grids, cards, timelines) make evidence easier to scan.
  • Small, consistent notes after each milestone reduce end-of-term stress.
  • Clear naming and file structure saves time for both teacher and student.

Strengths

What is working well.

  • Clear communication of goals and steps.
  • Organized files and predictable structure.
  • Ability to synthesize complex tasks into short summaries.
  • Positive attitude toward feedback and iteration.

Weaknesses

Things to improve next term.

  • Sometimes too much time on visuals instead of evidence depth.
  • Need quicker iteration on drafts before polishing.
  • More practice with time estimation for project steps.

Skills

Hard vs soft skills mapped clearly.

Hard Skills

  • Power Automate workflows
  • Power Apps prototyping
  • Cloud fundamentals and documentation
  • Project structure and evidence tracking

Soft Skills

  • Communication and clarity
  • Organization and prioritization
  • Team collaboration and feedback
  • Adaptability during challenges

Improvement plan

Specific next steps.

  • Write a short evidence log after each project session.
  • Prototype first, then refine design once content is complete.
  • Schedule weekly review to track goals and adjust priorities.
